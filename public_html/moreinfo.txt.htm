Directions to Puget Ridge Cohousing

Directions from the West Seattle Freeway

  • Go west on the West Seattle freeway
  • Take second exit (Delridge/South Seattle Community College)
  • Follow the ramp to the left and under the freeway
  • Go south on Delridge about 1.75 miles
  • At Orchard/Sylvan Way, make left
    • this intersection has two gas stations and a self storage
    • if you pass Kmart, you've gone 1 block too far
  • On Orchard, make first left (called Dumar Way)
  • Drive to traffic circle and make right on Myrtle Street
  • Make first left onto 17th
  • Go past first parking garage entrance on your left and park
  • Enter site between the two parking garages
  • A diagram of the site is on your right
    • Welcome to Puget Ridge Cohousing!
