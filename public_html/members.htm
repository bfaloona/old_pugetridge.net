Puget Ridge Cohousing online calendar and documents

Overview

Basic info about using community email and email lists, official community events, as well as a few key community documents should all be accessible on-line, password protected. MMC and Meals committee could update the calendar and anyone in the community could reference it easily, at any time. Information about what email lists exist and who is on which committee could be shared in an easy to remember location.

Examples:
  • Login to https://members.pugetridge.net (under construction) as google@pugetridge.net to link to the calendar, documents, etc.
  • Login to http://calendar.pugetridge.net as google@pugetridge.net to view the calendar.
  • Login to the calendar as meals@pugetridge.net to update the meals schedule, or the Young Families meal schedule.
  • Login to https://docs.pugetridge.net to upload the latest phone list, or download a printout of the chore team schedule.
  • Login to https://members.pugetridge.net as admin@pugetridge.net to create a simple webpage to answer a common question about how to use pugetridge email or search for documents, etc.

Users:

  • admin@pugetridge.net (network folks)
  • mm@pugetridge.net (maintenance management)
  • clc@pugetridge.net (community life)
  • meals@pugetridge.net
  • any community member with a google account can be invited to view and edit documents or calendar
