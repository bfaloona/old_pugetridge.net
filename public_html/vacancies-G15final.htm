UNIT FOR SALE

Beautiful spacious unit is available for sale in well-established Puget Ridge Co-housing Community. South-facing, two-story condo with ground floor access and attached deck. Soundly built wood-frame construction end-unit in a 3-unit building with one common wall. The unit has one dedicated garage space. Total of 1200 square feet.

Downstairs has open living/dining/kitchen area, one bedroom and one bath. Hardwood floors throughout with painted steel stringer/hardwood tread open staircase to second floor. Large upstairs room allows for multipurpose use such as a reading room, office or second bedroom and has adjacent storage room or future remodel potential.

Windows allow lots of natural light and there are electrically operated skylights and high ceiling fan. Custom top-down, bottom-up, honeycomb shades on windows.

The unit includes gas stove, refrigerator and dishwasher. Gas hot water heater and forced air heating. Pantry for W/D hookup or use shared community laundry facilities.

Many common Community amenities including; natural and landscaped setting on approximately 2-acres, garden spaces, guest room, well-equipped shop, adult/child recreation areas, and large dining/kitchen facilities. The Puget Ridge Community is supportive, active and with people of all ages as your neighbors.

This West Seattle location is an easy commute to downtown Seattle, SeaTac Airport and South Seattle Community College with good bus service close.

Offered at $332,000.
